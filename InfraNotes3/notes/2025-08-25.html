Writing notes

Before Class

  • Look at previous notes
  • Look at the syllabus/course outline

During Class

  • Brief
  • Main Ideas
  • Personal/Own words
  • Leave -> Leave space to write in the future
  • Ask/Write down my questions

Post/After Class

  • Review Notes
  • Format it to understand and rememver concepts
  • Write it online

Important

  • Be consistent with style
  • Always take notes
  • Watch the videos in the course presentation
